# How to Register Your CNDA2 Account

## Overview

CNDA is migrating to a new system (CNDA2) that uses WashU Key for authentication instead of local usernames and passwords.

All users must create a new CNDA2 account.

---

## Before You Start

- You must have a **WashU Key**
- Your CNDA2 account will be created **from scratch**
- Your existing CNDA access will be re-linked by the CNDA Help Desk

---

## Step 1: Obtain a WashU Key (if needed)

- WashU faculty and staff already have a WashU Key
- External users must apply for one

To apply:

1. Complete the WashU Key guest request process
2. Use the following values when prompted:
   - Duration: 6–12 months  
   - Visiting location: N/A  
   - Purpose: *Electronic access to cnda.wustl.edu*
3. Enter the email of a WashU faculty sponsor (typically your PI)

You will receive an email when your key is approved.

Note:
- CNDA Help Desk does not manage WashU Keys
- Your sponsor can renew or revoke access if needed

---

## Step 2: Create Your CNDA2 Account

1. Go to: https://cnda2.wustl.edu  
2. Click **WUSTL Connect**

Important:
- Do not enter anything in USER or PASSWORD fields  
- Do not click Register or Forgot Login  

3. Log in with your WashU Key  
4. Complete two-factor authentication if prompted  

---

## Step 3: Complete Registration

After login, you will be redirected back to CNDA:

1. Ensure **New CNDA Account** is selected  
2. Review the generated Username  

Recommendation:
- Use your existing CNDA username if possible  

3. Click **Submit**

---

## Important: Name Autofill Issue

Some browsers may autofill your name incorrectly:

- **First Name and Last Name may be swapped**

Before submitting:
- Verify that:
  - First Name is correct  
  - Last Name is correct  

If needed, manually correct them before submitting.

---

## Step 4: Notify CNDA Help Desk

After creating your account:

- Contact the CNDA Help Desk  
- Provide your new username  

They will:
- Link your account to existing projects  
- Restore your access

---

## Step 5: Wait for Migration Completion

After registering:

- Do not begin normal work in CNDA2 yet  
- Wait for the official migration announcement  

You will be notified when the system is ready for use.

---

## Notes

- Your old CNDA account is not transferred automatically  
- Project access must be re-linked  
- Scripts and XNAT Desktop Client access require alias tokens (see separate guide)
